Commencement Updates

  • Columbia University Commencement will be held on Wednesday, May 20, 2009. School ceremonies and events will take place from Saturday, May 16 through Thursday, May 21, 2009. This Web site is updated regularly. Please check back often for new information.

  • On May 20, 2009, the lights on New York City's Empire State Building will illuminate the building in blue and white-the Columbia colors-in honor of Columbia University's 255th Commencement Ceremony.

  • To view the webcasts of the 2009 University Commencement or Class Day ceremonies, please go to the Webcast Archive Page.
