Columbia University is committed to attracting, encouraging, and retaining a highly qualified workforce to support our mission of excellence in education and research. A world-renowned research university located in New York City, Columbia's success is the result of a diverse workforce coming together to help shape the future.

With 5,000 full-time officers of instruction and research and 8,000 full-time administrative staff, Columbia University is one of the top employers in the City of New York. We have two principal campuses: the historic campus in Morningside Heights, at 116th Street and Broadway, and the modern Medical Center further uptown, at 168th Street and Broadway. The University also encompasses the Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory in Palisades, New York, Nevis Laboratories, in Irvington, New York, and offices and centers at several off-site locations.

Visit the A-Z Index to learn more about our schools, colleges, libraries, and administrative departments.

For those of us who work at Columbia, there's more to the University than work. Columbia offers excellent employee benefits, and it is itself a center of learning and culture.

For information about openings at Columbia University please view our online employment application systems. Jobs at Columbia (JAC) lists administrative and staff positions. Recruitment of Academic Personnel System (RAPS) is a listing of current openings for academic officers, including instructional, research, and clinical faculty and officers, as well as officers of the libraries, intercollegiate athletics coaches, and teachers at the K-8 school. Both sites are available 24 hours daily for viewing and applying for positions.

Columbia University is an equal opportunity/affirmative action employer. Learn more.
